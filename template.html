📥 Evidence Collection Steps

  1. Filter to ✅ Can Close tab — these APMs have MAR evidence ready
  2. Click 📥 CSV to download the MAR evidence file
  3. Click 🔗 AB to open the finding in AuditBoard
  4. Upload the CSV as evidence attachment in AuditBoard
  5. Check ✅ Done — the row moves to the "Collected" sub-tab
